About MCAST

Mission Statement:
“To provide universally accessible vocational and professional education and training with an international dimension, responsive to the needs of the individual and the economy.”

Established in 2001, the Malta College of Arts, Science and Technology is the country’s leading vocational education and training institution. With six institutes in Malta and the Gozo Campus, MCAST offers over 190 full-time and 300 part-time courses ranging from certificates to Doctoral level (MQF Level 1 to Level 8).

These include the Institute of Applied Sciences, the Institute for the Creative Arts, the Institute of Engineering and Transport, the Institute of Business Management and Commerce, the Institute of Community Services, and the Institute of Information and Communication Technology.

MCAST’s strong relationship with industry ensures its courses are flexible, relevant, and responsive to real-world demands. Through its Apprenticeship Programme, entrepreneurship initiatives, and its commercial arm MG2i, the college supports small and medium-sized enterprises and develops tailored training programmes.

MCAST continues to expand by offering Lifelong Learning programmes, accrediting professional development courses, and growing its international partnerships through MG2i.
